# Everyday Support for Newcomers in Germany

This app helps people who are new to Germany find essential information for daily life – from doctors and lawyers to government offices, housing, schools, and more.  

---

## About Us

Welcome to our app for people who are new in Germany!  
Here, you can quickly and easily find all the important information for everyday life: from doctors and lawyers to government offices, housing, schools, leisure activities, and much more.  

We aim to help you feel confident and informed as you navigate your new environment, make important decisions, and organize your daily life.  
Our app is designed to guide, inform, and support you – making your start in Germany easier, clearer, and more independent.

---

## Features

- Find trusted information about doctors, lawyers, and authorities  
- Assistance with housing, schools, and other essential services  
- Access to leisure activities and social opportunities  
- Clear and easy-to-use navigation  
- Multilingual content (optional)
